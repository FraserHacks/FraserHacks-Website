Graphics Designer - FraserHacks

Description

We are looking for a Graphics Designer to help us create graphics and illustrations for the next iteration of FraserHacks.


You will work with the Web Development, Sponsorship, and Social Media teams to design graphical assets as required.


What you'll be working on:

You will be working with various teams to help make FraserHacks a success! Your main responsibilities will include redesigning the FraserHacks logo, creating graphics/illustrations for our various digital properties (Instagram, website, etc.), and assisting in the design of a sponsorship package.


Most of this work will need to be done on your own free time. There will also be occasional meetings to discuss developments and changing requirements.


Depending on the number of qualified applicants we receive, you may be working by yourself, or with another graphic designer as a team.


Requirements

Our ideal candidate has some, or all of these qualities:

  • Driven, capable, and eager to learn
  • Experience with graphic design using software such as Adobe Illustrator
    • Logos
    • Illustrations
    • Graphics for Social Media
    • Anything else you think is relevant
  • Comfortable working with an agile team to meet deliverables within a set time frame
  • A passion for growing their skillset and helping their peers
  • The ability to take responsibility for their own projects, and improve upon feedback
  • Experience with prototyping tools (Figma, Sketch, InVision, etc.) a plus
  • Experience with logo/brand identity design a plus
  • Dribbble/Behance Profile a plus
