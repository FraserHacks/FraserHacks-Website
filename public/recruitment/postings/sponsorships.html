Sponsorships - FraserHacks

Description

FraserHacks is a large event with over 150 students, teachers, and volunteers projected to be in attendance. As a member of the sponsorships team, you will mainly be responsible for helping acquire funding from our Hackathon.


We are looking for people who are motivated to help other teens further their passion in coding, and are able to communicate effectively in a more formal setting.


What you'll be working on:

You will be a direct contributor towards the success of FraserHacks 2020!


Your responsibilities will include:

  • Helping us create a sponsorship package
  • Liaising with sponsors and mentors
  • Managing the changing requirements of various sponsors
  • Helping us acquire funding from various sponsors


Requirements

Our ideal candidate has some, or all of these qualities:

  • Able to communicate within a business environment
  • Great writing and communication skills
  • Prior experience working in teams
  • Self-driven, and able to take responsibility for certain projects.
  • Connections with potential sponsors a plus
  • Prior experience on with sponsorships or logistics a plus
