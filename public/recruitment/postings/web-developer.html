Web Developer - FraserHacks

Description

We are looking for a Web Developer to help us build the next iteration of our FraserHacks website, and to meet any other digital needs that may be required.


You will help us deliver a delightful digital experience across our digital properties, ensuring that hackathon registration is seamless and intuitive.


What you'll be working on:

You will be responsible for working with other developers and designers to produce an eye-catching and well-designed website.


Most of this work will need to be done on your own free time. There will also be occasional meetings to discuss developments and changing requirements.


You will also take ownership of certain projects that will enhance our digital presence, and act as a direct contributor in an agile team.


Requirements

Our ideal candidate has some, or all of these qualities:

  • Driven, capable, and eager to learn
  • Preferably experienced with building well-designed and responsive websites
  • Prior experience with HTML/CSS/JS
  • Comfortable working with an agile team to meet deliverables within a set time frame
  • A passion for growing their skillset and helping their peers
  • The ability to take responsibility for their own projects, and improve upon feedback
  • Experience with prototyping tools (Figma, Sketch, InVision, etc.) a plus
  • Experience with React and Gatsby a plus
  • Experience with any Javascript based frameworks a plus
  • Experience with accessibility and a11y standards a plus
  • Experience with Circle CI/Jest a plus
