Logistics - FraserHacks

Description

FraserHacks is a large event! As such, we are looking for hardworking and motivated individuals to help us organize and host FraserHacks.


We understand that applicants may have other priorities, but we ask that you are able to work at least 2 hours a week to complete your assigned task(s) and completely prioritize FraserHacks near the event launch date to ensure that everything is working smoothly.


What you will be working on

You'll help us choose from a myriad of event venues, work with John Fraser administrative staff to deal with liability issues, order meals and snacks, schedule the event and organize workshops.


While meetings to discuss progress and need for collaboration between assignees will be held occasionally, the bulk of the work will require you to complete it in your own free time.


Requirements:

  • Good teamwork and communication skills are a must-see
  • Planning and organizational skills
  • The ability to take ownership of certain projects (i.e. Food, Signups, etc.) and deliver effectively
  • Experience organizing and hosting large-scale (150+ people for over 8 hours!) events a plus
